Tuesday

After 29 days and 29 nights I am free. Fasting is deceptive. It seems easy at first, but many realize that the real challenge is continuing to fast for around 30 days. I took for granted being able to indulge myself with food. Never again will I mock those who are emanciated. I don’t have the faintest idea of what else I could write about, but I hope that over these next few weeks, I ameliorate my writing skills and learn to express myself more.

Today I learned that hunger is more than than a biological deficit, it also affects the mind.
